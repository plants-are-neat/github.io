Meet Our Team

We are the Proud Shark-Tank Winners

While we do not have sharks, we know that we are great in our hearts. We continue to show that through the development of an ever more resilient mesh network system.

Ben Lively, head of Electrical Engineering and power systems, constructed the solar recharging mechanism and the modular custom PCB sensor design.

Emanuel Parez and Sergio Pareja are our lead mesh network professionals. They have built the mesh protocol working as the backbone of our communication system.

Maxine Lobes leads our environmental insolation effort. Mainly the objective of ensuring the technology can exist in the real world.

Noah Spahn is our almost mature full-stack developer. They build the structure of the data storage and visualization as well as some funny front-end jokes.
